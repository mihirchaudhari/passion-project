Why is Coding my Passion?

For Me, Coding is a Medium

Some people say that coding is the closest thing we have to a superpower, and I believe that. Coding turns one's imagination into something that other people can experience. Although learning a coding language may be considered a skill, coding itself is not a skill. Coding shows how we think and solve problems. This is one of the other reasons why I love coding, I get to solve problems and express my imagination. I found that I can code anytime I want and I always enjoy doing it, when you have this feeling about something, that is how you know that it's your passion.

What can Code do?

Asking what code can do today is a question asked without much thought. In this generation(21st century), we have now adapted ourselves to this technology much more advanced than the previous generations can handle. You could say that code has taken over the world, and we do need it a lot. Not only for the applications in our devices, but also to the appliances we use. How do microwaves know how many seconds to warm something, how do printers know if to do a print, a scan, or a fax? We rely on code now a lot, but we also rely a lot on other subjects, because whatever your passion may be, it is important. We need people who can serve us food at fast food restaruants and people who can cut our hair. Your passion may seem random, but as long as you know it's your passion, follow it!
